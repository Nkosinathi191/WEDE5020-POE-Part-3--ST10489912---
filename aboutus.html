About Us

Empowering voices. Celebrating stories. Connecting readers worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by a group of passionate writers and editors in Cape Town, Trivest Publishing House was born out of a dream: to create a platform for young voices often overlooked in mainstream publishing.

What started as a small independent press has grown into a publishing house reaching readers across South Africa and beyond. Our commitment is to bring fresh, bold, and diverse stories to life.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To publish powerful stories written by young people of colour that move, inspire, and challenge readers.
  • Vision: To become a leading publishing house for emerging indie authors in Africa and worldwide.
